Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SHARE Bear We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy within the Baylor community ecosystem. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for the waitlist or use the platform:

  • Account Information: Name, Baylor email address (@baylor.edu), and academic classification.
  • Marketplace Data: Items you express intent to buy or sell.
  • Authentication: Data verified through Baylor Single Sign-On (SSO).

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to facilitate the peer-to-peer sustainable marketplace:

  • To secure and verify your identity as an active Baylor student.
  • To facilitate communication between buyers and sellers.
  • To notify you of platform updates, waitlist status, and marketplace activity.

4. Data Sharing & Security

SHARE Bear is an exclusive community platform. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your data is shared only with other authenticated Baylor users strictly for the purpose of completing marketplace transactions.

We implement appropriate technical security measures (including Supabase encryption) to protect your data. However, no electronic transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact our team at uif@baylor.com.
